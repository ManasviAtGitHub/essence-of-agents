CORTEX · the model
An LLM is, near enough, a pure function: context in, a distribution over next tokens out. It keeps no memory between calls unless you put it there. So building an agent is two jobs: deciding what goes into the context, and turning some of what comes out into actions. That is exactly what Bit does for Cortex.
