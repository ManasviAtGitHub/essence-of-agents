Every number here is a Value that tracks its own gradient (autograd.py); the model is embed → one causal attention head → MLP → logits (model.py); training is a random window, cross-entropy against the next token, backward(), then SGD (train.py) - about 900 weights on a two-line corpus. Real models are this exact math, scaled a trillion-fold. Nothing leaves your browser.
