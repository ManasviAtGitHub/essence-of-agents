Serve many

TRACK 5 . S5 . verified as of 2026-07 - keep the GPU full, pack the cache

S4 served one user in one tab. A datacenter serves thousands - on the SAME expensive GPU. The trick is packing: read the weights once and serve a whole BATCH of users per step, and never let the GPU idle. Two mechanisms do it - continuous batching (keep the griddle full) and paged attention (pack the KV cache like lockers).
